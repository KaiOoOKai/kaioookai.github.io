CPSC 583 Group 18 - Global Food Prices Comparison

Our Data Set

WFP (The World Food Program) Collected Global Food Prices from 1993 to 2021. We Used a subset of this data starting from 2003 with 32 food types.

Link to Dataset: Here

Research Questions

  1. How does the number of markets affect the price of commodities in a region?(Cartogram)
  2. Will food in the same country be similar in price?(Stacked Bar Chart)
  3. How does the type of food commodity (food type) affect the price?(Stacked Bar Chart)
  4. How much does a particular food commodity change in price over a given timeline? (Line Chart)

Main Visualization Here

Navigation instructions:

  1. Click the link above (Main Visualization Here)
  2. To get to the stacked bar, make sure you're in the view of "Number of Markets in Each Region"
  3. Click any coloured region (grey regions mean there is no data)
  4. When viewing the stacked bar, you can view the prices over a set of years when you click on the "View Prices Over The Years" button

To view the best visualization, follow these steps:

  1. In the Cartogram, click on the country Philippines
  2. A stackbar will be brought up, now click "View Market Prices Over The Years"
  3. Select the market "La Trinidad" in the 'Market' dropdown
  4. In the Food Type dropdown, select
    • - Cabbage
    • - Carrots
    • - Tomatoes
    • - Eggs
    • - Pork
