CPSC 583 Group 18 - Global Food Prices Comparison

Dataset: Here

WFP (The World Food Program) Collected Global Food Prices from 1993 to 2021. We Used a subset of this data starting from 2003 with 32 food types. Here are some visualizations we've created:

  • Cartogram
  • Stacked Bar Chart
  • Line Chart

Research Questions

  1. How does the number of markets affect the price of commodities in a region?(Cartogram)
  2. Will food in the same country be similar in price?(Stacked Bar Chart)
  3. How does the type of food commodity (food type) affect the price?(Stacked Bar Chart)
  4. How much does a particular food commodity change in price over a given timeline? (Line Chart)
